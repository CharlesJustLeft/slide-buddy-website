How much does it cost?

Slide Buddy is 100% FREE. No subscription fees, no hidden costs. Just install and start translating!

Can I undo translations?

Yes! All changes work with Google Slides‚Ñ¢ native undo. Just press Ctrl+Z (or Cmd+Z on Mac).

How do I translate just one slide?

Navigate to the slide you want to translate, open Slide Buddy, and say "translate this slide to [language]" or "translate current page to [language]".

What languages are supported?

30+ languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese, Russian, Arabic, Italian, and many more!
