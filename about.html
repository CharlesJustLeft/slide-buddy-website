Why I Built Slide Buddy

Working with international teams, I saw firsthand how painful it was to manually translate presentation decks. People were spending hours copying and pasting text into Google Translate, slide by slide.

I thought: "There has to be a better way."

So I built Slide Buddy — a free, AI-powered tool that translates entire presentations in seconds. No subscriptions, no hidden fees, just a simple solution to a common problem.

"If I can save someone even 30 minutes of tedious work, that's a win in my book."

— My motivation for every project
