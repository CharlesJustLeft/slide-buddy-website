Best Free Google Slides Translation Extensions (2025 Comparison)

CZ
Charles Zuo
• Nov 21, 2025 • 5 min read

Translating Google Slides presentations manually is time-consuming and often results in broken formatting and inconsistent translations, leaving users frustrated with hours of tedious work.

To solve this problem, we tested four of the most popular translation add-ons—Translate My Slide, Slides Translator (Brown), Slides Translator (Purple), and Slide Buddy—on the same task of translating a single slide into Spanish, comparing their usability, speed, accuracy, and overall user experience.

Evaluation Criteria Breakdown

When assessing translation extensions, several critical metrics determine their effectiveness and user satisfaction. The key evaluation factors include:

  • User Interface (UI) design - measuring how clean and aesthetically pleasing the interface appears
  • User Experience (UX) - evaluating how intuitive and consistent the workflow feels during actual use
  • Translation Speed - the time required to translate a single slide
  • Translation Quality - examining whether the output maintains original styling, layout integrity, and linguistic accuracy
  • Pricing Structure - free versus trial versus paid options
  • Formatting Preservation capabilities
  • Language Coverage breadth
  • Natural Language/AI Integration capabilities that leverage advanced artificial intelligence models
  • Google Workspace Integration functionality

Individual Extension Reviews

Translate My Slide

Translate My Slide demonstrates solid performance with over 2 million installations and a 4.1-star rating, offering a straightforward approach to presentation translation using Google Translate API. The extension provides daily translation quotas in its free version, with premium plans available for unlimited usage, and supports over 100 languages while maintaining a modern, user-friendly interface that appeals to educators and businesses.

Slides Translator (Brown)

Slides Translator (Brown) dominates the market with over 40 million installations, making it the most widely adopted solution despite its 3.6-star rating. This extension supports comprehensive translation capabilities including text, images, and speaker notes, powered by Google Translate, DeepL, and Gemini APIs, though users report a cluttered dropdown interface that can feel confusing during workflow.

Slides Translator (Purple)

Slides Translator (Purple) offers a more limited experience with 742,000 installations and requires manual copy-paste operations that frequently break formatting, making it suitable primarily for occasional use with its 5 free trial limit.

Slide Buddy

Slide Buddy represents a unique approach in the translation extension market. Despite maintaining only 313 installations, this tool has earned a perfect 5.0-star rating from users who appreciate its AI-powered natural language interface. The extension stands out by remembering user preferences and delivering remarkably fast translations in approximately 7 seconds while preserving formatting with exceptional accuracy.

Choosing the Right Tool

For users seeking the best free overall solution, Slide Buddy stands out with its AI-powered interface and zero restrictions, while those preferring established options with proven track records should consider Translate My Slide's 2+ million user base and reliable performance.
